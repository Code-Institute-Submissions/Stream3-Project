We work from our workshop on Albert Road, Southsea. Your always welcome to pop in and discuss your requirements

We are Sigita and Jola, a two lady team, who are both highly experienced tailors and dressmakers with the skills to repair or alter any garments including unusual fabrics. We work with leather, fine silk, PVC, fur etc. We have over 30 years of experience between us and this means we can literally handle anything from replacing a zip on your favourite jeans, fixing up a bust rucksack or altering a Chanel gown. We also undertake dressmaking projects.

As part of our quality service, we offer fast, same day and emergency services for simple repairs or alterations, in addition to providing free local pick up and drop off service for a small charge.

We offer very reasonable prices for the local area and if you already have a quote we will strive to beat it. We consider no task is too small or too big, so please contact us and let us provide advice and a free no obligation quote.
